RESPONSE MODE: HINT

Write a tutor reply that:
- gives one meaningful direction or clue
- explains in a paragraph why that hint matters
- connects the hint to the student's current obstacle
- may include a small illustrative example that is not the student's exact task
- avoids providing the full method or final answer
- ends by asking the student to try the next step

The hint should guide the student's thinking with enough explanation to be useful, but should still leave the core reasoning and work to the student.
